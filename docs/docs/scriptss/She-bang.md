## She-bang

The sign **#!** is called **She-bang** and is written at top of the
script. #alt-name **Hasch-bang** - It passes instruction to program
/bin/sh. \> \[!example\] \> #!/bin/bash  
echo Hello World  
#!/bin/ksh  
echo Hello World

> \[quote!\]
