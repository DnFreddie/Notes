## Commands
  • blkid list all the devices that are being in used currently
  • lbsk list block devices
    • -e to exclude things like snap
  • lsscsi It displays a list of SCSI devices connected to the system, (such as disk, CD/DVD drive, or tape drive)
  • fdisk tool to partiont
  • fcstat Show the fiber connected devices
  • resize2fs to resize the partiton(without arguments it fills up the entire partion)
