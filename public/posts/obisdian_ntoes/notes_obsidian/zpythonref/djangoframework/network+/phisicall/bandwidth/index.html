Bandwidth

#Constant The maximum amount of data that can be transmitted over a network connection in a given amount of time.

  • It is typically measured in bits per second (bps) or a multiple thereof, such as kilobits per second (Kbps), megabits per second (Mbps), or gigabits per second (Gbps).
